The "No Side Bearings" values cannot be compared, since the side bearings may have been changed by Autokern. Likewise, the "Kerning" values are relative to values that may be different.

Instead, the key value to look at here is the "Extrema offset difference." It is the difference between the "extrema offsets", ie. the difference between the x-extrema of the two glyphs. Although not significant in and of themselves, the "extrema offsets" are independent of the side bearings and reflect the distance between the glyph contours.
